= Sandor Lehoczky =
Sandor Lehoczky , who is from Alabama, helped start the Mandelbrot Competition and co-wrote the Art of Problem Solving Volume 1 and Art of Problem Solving Volume 2 .  He is married with three children and works  for Jane Street Capital in New York City.
