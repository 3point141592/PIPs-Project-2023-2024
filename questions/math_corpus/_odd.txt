= Odd integer =
An odd integer   $ n $  is an integer which is not a multiple of  $ 2 $  (or equivalently one more than a multiple of  $ 2 $  ). The odd integers are  $ \ldots, -5, -3, -1, 1, 3, 5, \ldots. $  Every odd integer can be written in the form  $ 2k + 1 $  for some unique integer  $ k $  . The product of any two odd integers is odd and the result of a division where both the dividend and the divisor are odd is odd. But the sum and difference of any two odd integers are  even . The sum and difference of an even integer and odd integer are odd. Besides  $ 2, $  all  prime numbers are odd.
