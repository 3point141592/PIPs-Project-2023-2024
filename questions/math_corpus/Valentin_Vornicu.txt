= Valentin Vornicu =
Valentin Vornicu is a former Art of Problem Solving administrator and webmaster.  In 2002, he founded MathLinks , which merged with AoPS in 2004. Vornicu is a multiple time participant in the IMO for Romania. In 2006, Vornicu graduated from the University of Bucharest, and in 2008 he got his Masters degree in Algebra and Number Theory from the University of Bucharest. He was a coordinator for the IMO in 2005, 2006 and in 2008. He is also an instructor for WOOT . Vornicu also has 9 World Series of Poker gold rings and is currently the 486th ranked poker player in the world. Vornicu currently resides in San Diego.
