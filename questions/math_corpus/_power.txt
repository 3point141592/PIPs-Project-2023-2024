= Power =
Power is loosely defined as the speed something can do work . The SI unit for power is the Watt . 
== Formal Definition ==
Power is the amount of work that is done over a given period of time . It is also the derivative of work. If  $ P $  is power,  $ W $  is work, and  $ t $  is time, then: \[P=\frac{W}{t}\]
