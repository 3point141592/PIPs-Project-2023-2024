= Sam Vandervelde =
Sam Vandervelde is one of the founders of the Mandelbrot Competition and ran the competition until its completion in 2019.  He is also an instructor for the Worldwide Online Olympiad Training (WOOT) program.  He was an IMO silver medalist, a 2-time winner of the USAMO , a perfect scorer on the AIME , and a top-10 participant at National MATHCOUNTS .
