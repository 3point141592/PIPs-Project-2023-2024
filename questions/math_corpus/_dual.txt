= Dual =
A dual of a logical statement is a sort of inversion of the statement. 
== Rigorous definition ==
Consider a logic statement  $ Q $  . For each individual element or operator in  $ Q $  , we replace: Such a statement is the dual of the statement. An interesting fact of logic is that if a logical statement is true, then its dual is true.
