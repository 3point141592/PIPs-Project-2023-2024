= Primary Mathematics World Contest =
The Primary Mathematics World Contest (PMWC) is a contest which started in 1997, sponsored by the Po Leung Kuk foundation. It is designed for the middle school level, and consists of an individual round and a team round.  Each team consists of four people aged 13 or under. The competition itself takes place in Hong Kong. : 2-3 : 2.5-3.5 : 2.5-3.5 
== Structure ==
The problems are similar to AMC 10 / AMC 12 level, with an emphasis on number theory, geometry and logic.
