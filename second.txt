= Second =
The second is the SI unit for time . It used to be defined as the time in which light travels approximately 299792458 meters , or about 299792 kilometers . The current definition of a second is the time it takes for a cesium 133's radiation to oscillate for 9192631770 periods. This is assuming that the cesium atom is at 0 K.
