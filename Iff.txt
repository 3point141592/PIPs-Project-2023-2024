= Iff =
Iff is an abbreviation for the phrase "if and only if." In mathematical notation, "iff" is expressed as  $ \iff $  . It is also known as a biconditional statement. An iff statement  $ p\iff q $  means  $ p\implies q $   and   $ q\implies p $  at the same time.
