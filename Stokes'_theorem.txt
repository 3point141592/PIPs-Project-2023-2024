= Stokes' Theorem =
Stokes' Theorem is a theorem in calculus regarding the relationship between surface integrals and line integrals. 
== Statement ==
 $ \int_{S} \int \text{curl F} \cdot \text{dS}=\int_{C} \text{F} \cdot \text{dr} $
