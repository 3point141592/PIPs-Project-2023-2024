= Numerator =
The top number a fraction is called the numerator. For example, in the fraction  $ \frac{2}{3} $  ,  $ 2 $  is the numerator. In the fraction  $ \frac{3x^2}{5} $  ,  $ 3x^2 $  is the numerator.
